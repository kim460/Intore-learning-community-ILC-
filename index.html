About Us

Welcome to Intore Learning Community

Intore Learning Community is the guest and learning center of Agahozo-Shalom Youth Village. We welcome visitors who seek meaningful experiences while supporting a community dedicated to education, healing, and leadership development.

The Meaning of "Intore"

In Rwandan tradition, Intore refers to the chosen ones — young warriors known for their grace, strength, and dedication. Our name reflects our commitment to nurturing the next generation of leaders.

Located within the beautiful grounds of ASYV, our center provides a unique opportunity for guests to connect with Rwanda's story of resilience and hope while enjoying comfortable, purpose-driven hospitality.
